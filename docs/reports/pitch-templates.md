# Zavis Intelligence Reports — Press Pitch Templates

**Owner:** Zavis Intelligence press team (`press@zavis.ai`)
**Audience:** Zavis editorial + founder + ops when pitching reports to press
**Last updated:** 2026-04-11 (Item 6 build — Zocdoc roadmap)

These templates are the starter kit for pitching Zavis Intelligence Reports
to journalists and trade outlets. Three tiers:

1. **Tier 1 — UAE outlet** (The National, Gulf News, Khaleej Times,
   Arabian Business, Zawya, Gulf Business, Entrepreneur ME)
2. **Tier 2 — GCC regional** (Asharq Al-Awsat, Al Arabiya English,
   Al Bayan, Saudi Gazette, Qatar Tribune, Gulf Daily News BH,
   Oman Observer)
3. **Tier 3 — International healthcare trade** (Fierce Healthcare,
   STAT News, Modern Healthcare, Healthcare IT News, HIMSS Media,
   Becker's, Financial Times Health, Reuters Health)

Every pitch MUST include:
- A one-line hook = the report's headline stat
- A one-paragraph summary
- A clear attachment list (PDF, chart pack, dataset sample)
- A specific analyst name for interview (not a generic press line)
- Embargo window + release date
- The report page URL (live post-embargo)
- A single-click CTA (reply with "I'll cover this")
