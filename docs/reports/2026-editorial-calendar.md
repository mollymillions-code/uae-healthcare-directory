# Zavis Intelligence Reports — 2026 Editorial Calendar

**Owner:** Zavis Intelligence editorial + data team
**Source:** Zocdoc roadmap Item 6 (`docs/zocdoc-plans-reconciled.md`)
**Last updated:** 2026-04-11 (Item 6 build)

This calendar sequences the 10 UAE-specific report concepts from the
specialist research into a rolling 2026 release schedule with embargo
windows and press timing. Concepts match the exact slugs seeded by
`scripts/seed-reports.mjs` so the `/intelligence/reports/[slug]` route
class is populated end-to-end as soon as the editorial team lifts a
row from `status='draft'` to `status='published'`.

Rule of thumb:
- **One tentpole per month** in Q2–Q4 2026
- **T-10d:** draft lock
- **T-7d:** chart pack final
- **T-3d:** embargo pitch to tier-1 UAE outlets
- **T-2d:** embargo pitch to tier-2 GCC regional + tier-3 international
- **T-0:** public release, LinkedIn + X push, newsletter blast
- **T+1d:** follow-up to confirmed interested journalists
- **T+3d:** backlink + referring-domain tracking

## Monthly cadence

### Q2 2026 — Foundation

**May 2026 — UAE Healthcare Access Gap 2026 Edition (tentpole)**
- Anchor report for the year. Maps 12,519 DHA/DOH/MOHAP-licensed
  facilities against Dubai Pulse community polygons + Abu Dhabi Open
  Data neighborhoods.
- Press timing: Embargo T-3, release May 15.
- Target outlets: The National + Gulf News exclusive window, then
  Zawya + Gulf Business + Arabian Business, then STAT News +
  Financial Times Health.
- Paired with: a founder LinkedIn post + 5-tweet thread.

**June 2026 — Arabic-Language Doctor Shortage**
- Language-access audit. Fires on cultural + policy relevance.
- Press timing: Embargo T-3, release June 10.
- Target outlets: Asharq Al-Awsat + Al Arabiya English first,
  tier-1 UAE outlets second.
- Paired with: Arabic-language executive summary PDF.

**June mid-month micro: Zavis Methodology Deep Dive (companion piece)**
- Not a standalone report, a companion blog post on
  /intelligence/category/market-intelligence explaining how the
  Zavis corpus is built. Drives links into the reports hub.

## Workflow pre-flight per release

1. **T-14d:** Analyst brief locked, data pulled, draft outline signed off
2. **T-10d:** Draft body complete, methodology reviewed by
   data science, chart pack v1
3. **T-7d:** Chart pack finalised, PDF laid out, title + headline stat
   locked, SEO meta reviewed
4. **T-5d:** Report row `status` flipped from `draft` to `scheduled`
   in the `reports` table, PDF uploaded to R2, press one-pager
   finalised
5. **T-3d:** Embargo pitches to tier-1 UAE outlets go out
6. **T-2d:** Embargo pitches to tier-2 + tier-3 go out
7. **T-1d:** Tier-1 follow-ups (one reminder only)
8. **T-0 09:00 GST:** Report row flipped to `status='published'`,
   sitemap regenerated (ISR), public URLs live, LinkedIn + X push,
   newsletter blast
9. **T+1d:** Follow-ups to confirmed interested journalists
10. **T+3d:** Referring-domain + backlink capture logged in the
    Zavis SEO tracker
11. **T+7d:** Post-mortem — what worked, what didn't, update this
    calendar for the next cycle

## Success metrics per release

Track in Search Console + the Zavis SEO dashboard:

- Referring domains gained in 14 days (Zocdoc baseline: 80–250 per
  tentpole)
- Unique press mentions (coverage log)
- Downloaded PDF count
- Twitter/LinkedIn impressions
- GSC impressions on the report URL + hub URL
- GSC clicks on the report URL
- Anchor-text distribution in backlinks (watch for over-optimised
  exact-match anchors)
- Session time on the report page (target: ≥3 min)
