Adoption Challenges
What Holds Back AI Value
Data Fragmentation & Privacy

Siloed systems, inconsistent data lineage, and lack of unified governance. Privacy compliance adds complexity for cross-border data sharing.

Change Management Lag

Process redesign trails technical implementation. Cultural resistance to AI-driven decision-making slows adoption.

Talent Scarcity

Critical shortage of production ML skills. Over-reliance on international vendors leads to implementation delays.

Infrastructure Costs

High investment for compute resources. Business case uncertainty leads to hesitation without clear ROI.
