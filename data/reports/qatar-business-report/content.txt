AI Adoption Challenges — What Holds Back Value
Key barriers limiting AI implementation in the GCC region

Data Fragmentation & Privacy
Siloed systems, inconsistent data lineage, and lack of unified governance frameworks across organizations. Privacy compliance requirements add complexity for cross-border data sharing in the GCC.

Change Management Lag
Process redesign and organizational adaptation often trail behind technical implementation. Cultural resistance to AI-driven decision-making slows adoption in traditional business units.

Talent Scarcity
Critical shortage of production ML skills and AI engineers with regional context. Over-reliance on international vendors for specialized capabilities leads to implementation delays and knowledge gaps.

Infrastructure Costs
High investment requirements for compute resources and specialized platforms. Business case uncertainty leads to hesitation on capital allocation without clear ROI demonstration.
