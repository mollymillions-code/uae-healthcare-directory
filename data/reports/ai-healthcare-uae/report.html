Adoption Factors
What Drives — and Blocks — AI Adoption
KEY ENABLERS
Government Initiatives

UAE Vision 2031 and National AI Strategy with healthcare as a priority sector

Digital Infrastructure

98% internet penetration. World-class hospital IT systems ready for AI integration

Public-Private Partnerships

Oracle-Cleveland Clinic-G42 collaborations creating transformative AI ecosystems

MAIN BARRIERS
Data Privacy Concerns

77% of providers cite data protection as primary concern. Cross-border data flows remain complex

Implementation Costs

High initial investment required, especially challenging for smaller facilities and clinics

Skills Gap

56% of organizations report AI talent shortages. Competition from fintech and energy sectors

Dubai Health Authority integrates AI across all public hospitals. Mandatory AI training for staff.

2023

Clear guidelines for health data processing, cross-border transfers, and AI model training.

2025

New classification for AI medical devices. Streamlined approval for local AI diagnostics.
