AI Adoption Drivers & Barriers

Drivers
Government Vision
UAE's National Strategy for AI 2031 provides strong policy
support and funding for AI initiatives across sectors

Talent Ecosystem
Growing pool of AI specialists, tech-focused educational
programs, and favorable immigration policies for skilled
workers

Advanced Infrastructure
World-class digital infrastructure, high connectivity, and state-
of-the-art data centers supporting AI deployment

Competitive Pressure

remain relevant and meet customer expectations

Barriers
Implementation Costs
High initial investment for AI solutions, particularly challenging for
small and medium retailers

Data Privacy Concerns

requiring cultural shifts and employee upskilling
