Root Cause Analysis
Why Patients Don't Show Up
24%
Felt Better / Illness Resolved

Patients recover before their appointment date and do not bother to cancel, leaving the slot empty.

16%
Transport Difficulties

Distance, traffic, and limited public transit options in suburban areas create physical barriers to attendance.

15%
Inconvenient Time Slot

Rigid scheduling systems that do not adapt to patient preferences drive avoidable non-attendance.

12%
Cannot Leave Work

Employment constraints, especially among hourly workers, force patients to choose between work and care.

CRITICAL FINDING
Full-fee patients are 36% more likely to no-show

Patients paying full fee without insurance have significantly higher no-show odds compared to insured patients. The absence of a financial relationship with an insurer removes a key accountability mechanism.

ADDRESSABLE VS. STRUCTURAL

Over 67% of no-show reasons are addressable through technology: better scheduling flexibility, proactive reminders, and transport coordination. Only structural factors like sudden illness resist intervention.
